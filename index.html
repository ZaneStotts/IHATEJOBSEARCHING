About

Hello, my name is Zane! And I absolutely hate job searching.

After sending in such an overwhelming number of resumes, I started officially documenting my job search in October 2024.

I created this little website in order to help keep records, visualize data, publically share my progress, and shine light on the extreme difficulty of modern day job seeking.

My original goal was to find remote work in front-end development and web design. But at this point, I am incredibly defeated and willing to take anything with a sustainable wage.

I track six categories for each application, most of them being self-explanatory. Status is considered "under review" until a rejection email is received or one month passes with no update, at which it becomes a "ghosted" position.

Thank you for taking interest in my job searching journey!

Warning: This project is unfinished and currently under construction. Some features and functions do not yet work as intended.
