About

Hello, my name is Zane! And I absolutely hate job searching.

I created this little website to document the overwhelming number of applications I've submitted and publically share my progress.

My original goal was to find work in front-end development and web design. But at this point, I'm willing to take anything with a sustainable wage.

I track six categories for each application, most of them being self-explanatory. Status is considered "under review" until I receive a rejection email or one month passes with no update, at which it becomes "ghosted."

Thank you for taking interest in my job searching journey!
